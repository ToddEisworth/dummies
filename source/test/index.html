A construction scheme of topological spaces, which generalizes M. E. Rudin’s construction of a Dowker space in \(\zfc \), is given, and is shown to produce a proper class of Dowker spaces. A proper subclass of this class of spaces are provably collectionwise normal Dowker in \(\zfc \) alone. The theory \(\zfc +\ssh \), where \(\ssh \) is Shelah’s Strong Hypothesis, proves that the whole class consists of collectionwise normal Dowker spaces. Whether all members of this class are Dowker in \(\zfc \) is still open.
